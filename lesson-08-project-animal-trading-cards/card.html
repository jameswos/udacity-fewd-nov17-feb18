Baby Dragon

Baby Dragons hatch cute, hungry and ready for a barbeque.

  • Favourite Food: Anything spicy!
  • Damage Type: Area splash (he burps fire)
  • Music Preference: Latino heat!
  • Tips & Tricks: Use oven mitts when handling, just in case.

This fire-breathing hatchling is shy around other air units, but leave it alone and it will throw a fit! When not around other air units, Baby Dragons become enraged and gain bonus damage and attack speed.
